  • BACKGROUND OF THE PROJECT

We choose this topic because most of the accidents in Malaysia occur due to the drivers being ignorant regarding road safety. One of the attitudes that some of the drivers do are, not wearing seatbelt while driving their car. Besides that, the usage of smartphone while driving a car also can leads to road accidents involving the drivers on the road as they were not aware of their surroundings. There are many ways to reduce the number of road accidents in Malaysia.
